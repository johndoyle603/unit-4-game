SNACKS! A game by J.D.

Instructions:

Time to buy snacks at the ballgame! Unfortunately, the snack bar does not show any prices on the menu, and it gives you the bill before you even buy anything. Making matters worse, it only accepts exact change. Yeah, it's weird, I know...

The value of each snack is hidden until you click.

You win the game by matching the total price of your snacks to the bill. If your bill exceeds the predetermined bill, you lose.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each snack, and the total bill. Enjoy!
