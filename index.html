Ballpark food game!

Instructions:

Time to buy snacks at the ballgame! Sadly, the snack bar takes only exact change and does not show prices on the menu. Also, it gives you the bill before you buy anything.

Click a food item below to add cost to your total purchase.

You win the game by matching the total price of your snacks to the bill. You lose if your total bill exceeds the predetermined bill.

The value of each food item is hidden until you click.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each food item and the total bill. Enjoy!
