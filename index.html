Ballpark food game!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Below we have four yummy items from the concession stand. By clicking on a food item you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each food item is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each food item
